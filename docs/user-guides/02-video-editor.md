# AntFlow Video Editor Guide

AntFlow provides a powerful mobile video editing experience with two distinct modes: **Simple Editor** for quick edits and **Advanced Editor** for professional control.

## 1. Simple Editor
*Best for: Quick trimming, social media shorts, and simple montage.*

### Features:
-   **Clip Trimming**: Drag handles to shorten start/end times.
-   **Sequence Reordering**: Drag and drop clips to change their order.
-   **Basic Export**: Fast export to 1080p.

### How to Use:
1.  Navigate to **Simple Editor** from project creation.
2.  Tap "Add Media" to select videos/images from your gallery.
3.  Tap a clip to select it, then drag the yellow handles to trim.
4.  Tap "Export" to save the result.

## 2. Advanced Editor
*Best for: Multi-layer composition, effects, collaborations, and fine-tuning.*

### The Interface
-   **Preview Window**: Shows the current frame of your video. Toggle Play/Pause here.
-   **Timeline (Bottom)**: The heart of the editor.
    -   **Tracks**: Visual rows for Video, Audio, and Overlay layers.
    -   **Playhead**: The vertical line indicating the current time.
    -   **Cursors** (Collaboration): Colored vertical lines showing where other users are working.
-   **Toolbar (Center)**: Toggles for **Effects** and **Collaboration** panels.
-   **Control Bar (Bottom)**: Zoom controls (`+` / `-`) and the main **Export** button.

### Editing Actions
-   **Add Clips**: Tap the "+" icon on a track to import media.
-   **Select Clip**: Tap any clip in the timeline to select it (highlighted).
-   **Move Clip**: Long-press and drag a clip to move it along the timeline or between tracks.
-   **Split**: With the playhead positioned, tap the "Split" tool (if available) to cut a clip in two.

### Applying Effects
1.  Select a clip in the timeline.
2.  Tap the **Effects** button in the toolbar.
3.  Choose an effect type:
    -   **Filter**: Apply color grading presets (Cinematic, Vintage, B&W).
    -   **Adjustment**: Manually tweak Brightness, Contrast, and Saturation.
    -   **Speed**: Change playback speed (0.5x - 2.0x).
4.  Adjust parameters using the sliders and tap "Apply".

### Collaboration
*Work with your team in real-time.*
1.  Tap **Collaboration** in the toolbar.
2.  View **Active Users** currently in the project.
3.  See **Comments** left by teammates.
4.  **Real-time Cursors**: Watch as teammates move through the timeline—their cursors appear as colored lines.

## 3. Exporting Your Video

1.  Tap the **Export** button (green) in the bottom right corner.
2.  Wait for the rendering process to complete. A progress percentage will be displayed.
3.  **Post-Export Options**:
    -   **Share Screen**: Direct links to upload to YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram.
    -   **Save to Device**: Save the MP4 file to your local gallery.

## Tips for Success
-   **Auto-Save**: AntFlow saves your changes locally as you edit.
-   **Zoom In**: Use the zoom controls for precise frame-by-frame trimming.
-   **Performance**: For complex projects with many effects, use the "Proxy" mode (if enabled in settings) for smoother playback.
