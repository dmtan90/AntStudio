# Advanced Features Guide

Take your content to the next level with AntFlow's advanced AI and collaboration tools.

## 1. Voice Lab 🎙️
*Create custom AI voices and generate lifelike speech.*

### Accessing Voice Lab
From the **Dashboard**, tap **Voice Lab**.

### Voice Cloning
1.  Tap **"Clone New Voice"**.
2.  **Record Samples**: You will be prompted to read 3-5 short sentences. Ensure you are in a quiet environment.
3.  **Process**: Tap "Create Voice". The AI will analyze your timbre and tone.
4.  **Save**: Name your voice (e.g., "My Narrator Voice").

### Text-to-Speech (TTS)
1.  Go to the **TTS** tab.
2.  **Select Voice**: Choose from pre-made voices or your Cloned Voices.
3.  **Enter Text**: Type the script you want spoken.
4.  **Generate**: Tap "Generate Audio".
5.  **Use**: Download the MP3 or add it directly to a project timeline.

## 2. Collaboration 🤝
*Work together with your team on the same project.*

### Inviting Team Members
1.  Open a Project.
2.  Tap the **Share/Collaborate** icon in the header.
3.  Enter the email addresses of your team members.
4.  Set permissions: **Viewer** (Read-only) or **Editor** (Full access).

### Real-time Editing
-   **Live Sync**: Changes made by one user (e.g., trimming a clip) appear instantly for others.
-   **Presence**: See who is currently viewing the project via their avatar in the top bar.
-   **Cursors**: In the timeline, colored lines show exactly where your teammates are working.
-   **Comments**: Use the comment tool (in the Collaboration Panel) to leave feedback on specific timestamps.

## 3. Templates System 🎨
*Start faster with professional designs.*

### Using a Template
1.  Go to the **Templates** tab on the Dashboard.
2.  **Browse**: Filter by category (Intro, Vlog, Promo, TikTok).
3.  **Preview**: Tap a template to watch the demo.
4.  **Use**: Tap "Use Template". This creates a new project with the tracks, effects, and placeholders preload.
5.  **Swap Media**: Tap the placeholder clips in the timeline and choose "Replace" to insert your own footage while keeping the effects.

### Creating a Template (Pro)
1.  Open an existing project you want to save as a template.
2.  Go to **Settings > Save as Template**.
3.  Add tags and a category.
4.  Publish to your logic library or the public gallery.
