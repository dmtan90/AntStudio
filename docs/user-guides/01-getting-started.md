# Getting Started with AntFlow Mobile

Welcome to the AntFlow Mobile App user guide! This document will help you get set up and familiar with the core navigation.

## 1. Installation & Account Setup

### Prerequisites
- Android 10+ or iOS 14+
- Modern Webview (Chrome/Safari)
- Active Internet Connection (Wi-Fi recommended for initial sync)

### Creating an Account
1.  **Launch the App**: Open the AntFlow app on your device.
2.  **Sign Up**: Tap the "Sign Up" button on the welcome screen.
    - Enter your Email.
    - Choose a strong Password.
    - (Optional) Sign up with Google or Apple.
3.  **Authentication**: After signing up, you will be automatically logged in. 
    - *Note*: If you are logging in on a new device, check your email for a verification code if 2FA is enabled.

## 2. Dashboard Overview

Upon logging in, you will land on the **Dashboard**. This is your central hub for all activities.

### Key Sections:
-   **Header**: Displays your profile name and a quick "Logout" button.
-   **Quick Actions**:
    -   **Templates**: Access pre-made video templates for quick starts.
    -   **Analytics**: View your streaming performance and audience stats.
-   **Your Projects**: A list of your recent video projects. Tap a project to edit or view details.
-   **Bottom Navigation** (if applicable): Quick access to Home, Search, and Profile.

## 3. Creating Your First Project

1.  Tap the **"+" (Plus)** button or "New Project" card on the Dashboard.
2.  **Choose a Mode**:
    -   **Simple Editor**: Best for quick social media clips (Shorts/Reels).
    -   **Advanced Editor**: Full timeline editing for complex videos.
    -   **Live Stream**: Set up a broadcast.
    -   **Voice Lab**: Clone voices or generate TTS audio.
3.  **Project Details**: Give your project a name and description, then tap "Create".

## 4. App Settings

Tap your profile icon or the "Settings" gear to access:
-   **Profile**: Update your name, avatar, and password.
-   **Storage**: Manage local cache and synced files.
-   **Notifications**: Toggle push notifications for collaboration updates.
-   **Theme**: Switch between Dark (default) and Light mode.
