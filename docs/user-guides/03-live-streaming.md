# Live Streaming with AntFlow

AntFlow's Mobile Live Studio allows you to broadcast professional-grade streams directly from your device, featuring AI automation and multi-platform support.

## 1. Setting Up a Stream

1.  From the **Dashboard**, tap **Live Stream** or start a new "Live" project.
2.  **Stream Configuration**:
    -   **Title**: Enter a catchy title for your broadcast.
    -   **Description**: Add key details for your audience.
    -   **Privacy**: Choose Public (everyone), Unlisted, or Private.
3.  **Advanced Options**:
    -   **AI Director**: Toggle `ON` to let AI auto-switch scenes or highlight key moments (requires multiple camera sources).
    -   **Commerce**: Toggle `ON` to enable shoppable links (if configured).
4.  **Multi-Stream Destinations**:
    -   Select platforms to broadcast to simultaneously (YouTube, Twitch, Facebook).
    -   *Note*: Ensure your accounts are connected in **Settings > Platforms**.
5.  Tap **Go Live** to initialize the studio.

## 2. The Studio Interface

Once live, you enter the Studio Interface:
-   **Main Viewport**: What your audience sees.
-   **Camera Controls**: Switch between Front/Back cameras using the flip icon.
-   **Mic Toggle**: Mute/Unmute audio.
-   **Chat Overlay**: See incoming messages from all connected platforms in one unified feed.

## 3. During the Stream

-   **Monitoring**: Keep an eye on the "Live" indicator. If it turns yellow/red, your connection might be unstable.
-   **Interacting**: Tap a chat message to highlight it on stream (if enabled).
-   **Pause/Resume**: Use the pause button to temporarily stop video/audio transmission (shows a "Be Right Back" screen).

## 4. Analytics & Health

Swipe up or tap the **Analytics** button to view real-time stats:
-   **Viewer Count**: Current concurrent viewers across all platforms.
-   **Stream Health**:
    -   **Bitrate**: Target 4500+ kbps for 1080p.
    -   **FPS**: 30 or 60 fps.
    -   **Latency**: Lower is better (~1-3s for low latency mode).
-   **Engagement**: Total likes, comments, and shares.

## 5. Ending the Stream

1.  Tap the **Stop** (Red Square) button.
2.  Confirm "End Stream".
3.  **Post-Stream Report**: You will see a summary of your performance (Total Views, Duration, New Followers).
4.  The recording will automatically save to your **Projects** library for later editing/repurposing.
