# Advanced Web Features Guide

Unlock the full potential of AntFlow with our AI and Team tools.

## 1. Voice Lab (Web)
*Generate professional voiceovers directly in your browser.*

### Cloning a Voice
1.  Navigate to **Voice Lab** in the sidebar.
2.  Click **"New Clone"**.
3.  **Upload Samples**: Upload high-quality MP3/WAV files of the voice (at least 1 minute of clear speech).
4.  **Train**: Click "Start Training". This may take a few minutes.
5.  **Use**: Once ready, the voice appears in your "My Voices" list.

### Text-to-Speech Generation
1.  Select a **Voice** (Stock or Cloned).
2.  **Input Text**: Type or paste your script into the text box.
3.  **Settings**: Adjust Stability (consistency) and Similarity (emotion).
4.  **Generate**: Click the play button to preview.
5.  **Add to Project**: Click "Add to Library" to use it in the Video Editor.

## 2. Team Collaboration

### Workspace Management
-   **Switch Workspace**: If you belong to multiple teams, click the workspace name in the top left to switch contexts.
-   **Create Team**: Go to **Settings > Teams** to create a new organization.

### Managing Members
1.  Go to **Team Settings**.
2.  Click **"Invite Member"**.
3.  Enter email addresses.
4.  **Roles**:
    -   **Admin**: Full access to settings, billing, and all projects.
    -   **Editor**: Can create and edit projects.
    -   **Viewer**: Can only view published projects.

### Collaborative Editing
-   Open a project in the **Video Editor**.
-   When multiple members open the same project, you will see their **Avatars** in the top bar.
-   **Live Updates**: Changes are synced instantly.
-   **Locking**: If a user selects a clip, it is temporarily locked for others to prevent conflicts.

## 3. Brand Kit

Maintain consistency across your videos.
1.  Go to **Settings > Brand Kit**.
2.  **Upload Logos**: Add your company logos (PNG with transparent background).
3.  **Color Palette**: Define your primary, secondary, and accent colors.
4.  **Fonts**: Upload custom font files.
5.  **Usage**: These assets will be readily available in the "Brand" tab inside the Video Editor.
