# AntFlow User Manual

Welcome to AntFlow/AntStudio! This manual describes the features and workflows for end-users.

## 🌟 Introduction

AntFlow is an AI-powered video creation platform that allows you to turn text scripts, ideas, or products into cinematic videos automatically.

## 🎬 Dashboard Overview

Upon logging in, you will see the main Dashboard:
- **Recent Projects**: Quick access to your latest work.
- **Quick Actions**: "New Project", "Live Studio", "Assets".
- **Credit Balance**: Your remaining AI generation credits.

## 🛠️ Creating a New Project

1. **Click "New Project"**.
2. **Select Mode**:
   - **Script to Video**: Paste your script.
   - **Topic implementation**: Enter a prompt (e.g., "History of Coffee").
   - **Product Video**: Upload a product URL or image.
3. **Choose Settings**: Aspect ratio (16:9, 9:16), Style (Cinematic, Cartoon, Professional).
4. **Click "Generate"**.

The AI Director will analyze your input, create a storyboard, generate images/video clips, add voiceovers, and assemble the timeline.

## 🎞️ The Video Editor

The Editor provides professional control over the AI-generated content.

### Main Areas
- **Timeline**: Bottom area. Tracks for Video, Audio, Text, and Overlay.
- **Preview**: Center stage. Shows the current frame.
- **Sidebar**:
  - **Media**: Your uploads and generated assets.
  - **AI Lab**: Generate new images/videos/voices on the fly.
  - **Text**: Add titles and subtitles.
  - **Effects**: Filters and transitions.

### Common Actions
- **Trim**: Drag the edges of a clip on the timeline.
- **Split**: Move the playhead and click the "Split" (scissors) icon.
- **Replace**: Right-click a clip -> "Regenerate" or drag new media onto it.
- **Subtitle**: Click "Auto Subtitles" to generate captions from audio.

## 🤖 AI Features

### AI Director
The "God Mode" of the editor. It can automatically:
- Switch scenes based on pacing.
- Suggest B-roll footage.
- Re-write scripts for better flow.

### Voice Lab
- **Clone Voice**: Upload a 1-minute sample to create a digital twin.
- **Text-to-Speech**: Choose from 100+ premium voices.

## 📢 Publishing & Exporting

1. Click **Export** in the top right.
2. Choose Quality (720p, 1080p, 4K).
3. (Optional) Toggle "Publish to Socials" to auto-post to connected YouTube/Facebook accounts.
4. Download the final MP4 file.

## 🏢 Enterprise Features (If applicable)

- **Team Collaboration**: Invite members to your workspace.
- **Brand Kit**: Upload logos, fonts, and color palettes that the AI will enforce.
- **Approval Workflows**: Submit videos for manager review before publishing.

## ❓ FAQ & Support

**Q: How do I get more credits?**
A: Go to Settings -> Subscription to upgrade plan or buy credit packs.

**Q: Can I use my own footage?**
A: Yes, use the "Upload" tab in the Media sidebar.

For technical support, please contact your administrator or support@antflow.ai.
